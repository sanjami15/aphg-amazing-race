Cambodia may not be as developed now, but it definitely had an illustrious past. It was once home to the Khmer Empire, which covered almost all of the Indochina Peninsula. The Khmer capital was located at Angkor, where they built huge temple complexes like Angkor Wat and Angkor Thom. As the Khmer switched from Hinduism to Buddhism, so did the temples. To this day, it remains the world's largest Hindu temple. We've hidden various things related to Cambodia's history. Whichever team completes the scavenger hunt first wins!
