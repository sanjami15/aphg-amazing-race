Detour Challenge!

Since Incan times, Peru has been known for its camelids. Go to any part of its Central Andes and you are sure to find llamas, alpacas, and their wilder cousins, the guanacos and the vicuñas. So, to celebrate one of Peru's best artisanal traditions, our contestants will help shear and sort alpaca wool, which will be used to make beautiful and warm clothes.
